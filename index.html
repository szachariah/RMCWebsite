Illinois Institute of Technology

We are the Scarlet Space Hawks from the Illinois Institute of Technology. We are a robotics team that competes annually in the NASA's Robotic Minning Competition at the NASA Kennedy Space Center at Cape Canaveral, FL. Along with Besides from building robots, we also participate in outreach events to encourage young students to pursue STEM within our Chicago community!
