Illinois Institute of Technology

We are the Scarlet Space Hawks from the Illinois Institute of Technology. We are a robotics team that competes annually in the NASA's Robotic Minning Competition at the NASA Kennedy Space Center at Cape Canaveral, FL. Besides from building robots, we also participate in outreach events to encourage young students to pursue STEM within our Chicago community!

Meet the 2017 Scarlet Space Hawks Team!

Team Structure

    I. Mechanical Team
    II. Electrical Electrical
    III. Autonomy Team

  • Each team is comprised of various disciplines from Illinois Tech!

    • Competition Objective

  • Build a fully-functioning mining robot that can traverse the aggressive Martian terrain.
  • Design a robot which can autonomously navigate and mine in the Martian soil.

    • Team Awards

  • Scarlet Space Hawks placed 3rd in the Mining Category (2017)
